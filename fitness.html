My Training Philosophy

My approach to fitness and athletic development is built around several core principles:

  • Consistency over intensity: Regular, sustainable training builds better results than sporadic intense sessions.
  • Focus on fundamentals: Mastering basic techniques creates a solid foundation for advanced skills.
  • Holistic development: Training should address technical skills, physical conditioning, mental focus, and tactical understanding.
  • Data-driven improvement: Tracking performance metrics helps identify strengths, weaknesses, and progress over time.
  • Community support: Sharing knowledge and experiences with other athletes creates motivation and accelerates growth.

Through my Instagram account @mj19_training.acl and this platform, I aim to inspire others to pursue their athletic goals while documenting my own journey of continuous improvement.
