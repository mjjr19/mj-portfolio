About Me

Combining technology expertise with athletic discipline

Product Manager & Data Analytics Professional

I'm a passionate Product Manager and Data Analytics professional with experience in developing data-driven solutions and managing successful products. Currently pursuing a Master of Science in Information Systems at the University of Washington's Foster School of Business, I combine technical expertise with leadership skills to deliver impactful results.

In my role at Arrahman Tours, I leverage Python and SQL to drive customer growth and retention through data analytics. I'm also experienced in leading teams, as demonstrated during my time as Vice President of the Muslim Student Association at the University of Washington.

Beyond my professional career, I'm an avid soccer player and fitness enthusiast. I run the training account @mj19_training.acl where I share soccer training routines, fitness tips, and lifestyle content. My passion for athletics complements my analytical mindset, bringing discipline and teamwork into all areas of my life.
